++# UX Review

## UX Strengths

### 1. Consistent Dark Theme

The platform maintains visual consistency across:

- Product pages

- Settings

- Checkout

- Cart

- Contact pages

Impact:

- Modern look

- Strong brand identity

- Good visual hierarchy

---

### 2. Discount Visibility

The pricing cards clearly show:

```
₹100 → ₹94
₹250 → ₹235
₹500 → ₹470
```

Benefits:

- Immediate value recognition

- Encourages impulse purchase

- Easy comparison

---

### 3. Flexible Payment Concept

FlexiPay allows custom amount selection.

Interesting because:

- Supports multiple budget ranges

- Reduces decision fatigue

- Unique differentiator

---

## UX Issues

### UX-001

#### Problem

FlexiPay amount field shows:

```
₹ 0
```

on page load.

Users may not understand:

- whether input is mandatory

- minimum amount

- what FlexiPay actually means

#### Recommendation

Show placeholder:

```
Enter amount between ₹250 - ₹10000
```

Priority: P1

---

### UX-002

#### Problem

"How To Redeem" section occupies significant vertical space.

Users must scroll before seeing products.

#### Impact

Product discovery delayed.

#### Recommendation

Collapse by default.

Priority: P2

---

### UX-003

#### Problem

No trust indicators near gift cards.

Missing:

- Verified seller

- Redemption guarantee

- Refund policy

#### Impact

Can reduce conversion.

Priority: P1
